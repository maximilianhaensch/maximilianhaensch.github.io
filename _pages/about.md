About me
======

I am currently a Master's student in [theoretical and mathematical physics](https://www.theorie.physik.uni-muenchen.de/TMP/index.html) in Munich. I am working in the [Quantum Field Theory group](https://www.mpp.mpg.de/forschung/aufbau-der-materie/quantenfeldtheorie) at the Max Planck Institute for Physics under the supervision of [Johannes Henn](https://www.mpp.mpg.de/ueber-uns/organisation/direktoren/prof-dr-johannes-henn). Before starting my Master's I spent one year at the [University of Washington in Seattle](https://phys.washington.edu) as a visiting graduate student. I completed my Bachelor of Science at the [Justus-Liebig-University](https://www.uni-giessen.de/de/fbz/fb07/fachgebiete/physik) in Giessen where I was supervised by [Fabian Rennecke](https://www.uni-giessen.de/de/fbz/fb07/fachgebiete/physik/institute/theorie/qft/people/rennecke).
This autumn I will start my PhD under the supervision of [Luis Fernando Alday](https://www.maths.ox.ac.uk/people/luis.alday) at the [University of Oxford](https://www.maths.ox.ac.uk/groups/mathematical-physics).


Research
======
I am generally very interested in non-perturbative aspects of quantum field theories and scattering amplitudes. In my Master's thesis I have looked at positivity properties in the coupling dependence of exact observables in planar maximally supersymmetric Yang-Mills. A [preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30368) of my results is available on arXiV. In the future I am looking forward to learning more about holography and conformal field theories.
